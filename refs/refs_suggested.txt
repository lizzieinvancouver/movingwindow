0) https://cran.r-project.org/web/packages/climwin/index.html

1) van der Pol et al. 2016 "Identifying the best climatic predictors in ecology and evolution" https://besjournals.onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.1111/2041-210X.12590 
	This paper describes the method. 

2) Bailey & van de Pol 2016 "climwin: An R Toolbox for Climate Window Analysis"  https://journals.plos.org/plosone/article?id=10.1371/journal.pone.0167980
	This paper also describes the method. 

3) Bailey et al. 2022 "Bird populations most exposed to climate change are less sensitive to climatic variation" https://www.nature.com/articles/s41467-022-29635-4
	Recent high profile paper using the method

4) Simmonds et al. 2019 "Cue identification in phenology: A case study of the predictive performance of current statistical tools" https://besjournals.onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/pdf/10.1111/1365-2656.13038

5) Tredennick et al. 2021 "A practical guide to selecting models for exploration, inference, and prediction in ecology" Overview of relevant terms and a decent take on how ecologists 'do' this sort of science.
